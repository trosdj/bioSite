Hobbies and Interests

Knitting

Violet spends a lot of her time knitting gifts for her family. Not one Trost will ever go with a cold head, or chilly hands, if she has anything to do with it. She enjoys both the feeling of finishing a project and how calming it can be. As Violet believes everyone can learn how to knit, click this card to learn how to knit from an online tutorial.

Family

Family is the most important thing to Violet. As the matriarch of the Trost family, she takes pride in the accomplishments of her children, grandchildren, and great grandchildren. She will often boast to everyone about their school sports, performances, and career advancements. Click this card to meet the Trost family.

Local Government

It was more than just a job for Violet. Taking care of her little town was something she was very happy to do. Across her long career, she made strides in making the Village of Vernon a bit of rural paradise. Click this card to learn more about Violet's journey working from with local government.
